Analysis

Music analysis is a pivotal step in the identification of a track's genre. Each genre possesses distinctive characteristics and features that set it apart. Our process involves a thorough analysis of these elements.

Classification

Post-analysis, our advanced Artificial Intelligence algorithm steps in to categorize the song accurately into its respective musical style. This classification hinges on an in-depth understanding of the analyzed musical features.

Insights

To shed light on the classification process, we provide data and graphical representations that portray the underlying structure and characteristics of the music. This deep-dive knowledge acquisition offers an enhanced comprehension of the composition.
